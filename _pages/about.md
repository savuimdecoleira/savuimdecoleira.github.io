**About me** 🌳
------
Forest Engineer graduated from the Federal University of Amazonas, currently pursuing a Master's degree in Remote Sensing at the National Institute for Space Research (INPE). Acts as a researcher at the TRopical Ecosystems and Environmental Sciences Lab (TREES), investigating the resilience of secondary Amazonian forests subjected to extreme droughts through the Amazon-SOS: A Safe Operating Space for Amazonian Forests project.

**My interests** 🌳
------
- 🛰️ Forest and ecological relationships analyzed through remote sensing products and data, especially in tropical regions
- 🗺️ Geotechnologies and remote sensing
- 🪵 Forest management and harvesting
- 🍃 Ecophysiology
